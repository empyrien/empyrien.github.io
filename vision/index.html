What if the moral code the age of AI is searching for has already been written?

A personal thesis: that the Bahá'í Writings — a hundred volumes centered on the oneness of humanity — speak directly to the century now asking machines to hold its values. Nothing here is pushed on you; the Faith's own first principle is the independent investigation of truth. Every claim is cited to source, and speculation is labeled as such. Choose a door.
